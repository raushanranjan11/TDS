Distribution Fee Payments.

Suppliers are required to pay transaction fees for distribution. These fees relate to reservation transaction, which are charged on a monthly basis to each individual supplier within a PNR (Passenger Name Record). When a single supplier provides all services within a PNR, only a single transaction fee will be charged. Please apply to TDS for current transaction fees.

Reservation transaction fees are billed to suppliers on a monthly basis by invoices transmitted via e-mail services in PDF format. Payment details will be included on each invoice including the applicable currency for international suppliers.
